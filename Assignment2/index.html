Exploring Australia's Climate

In this visual exploration, we delve into Australia's climate data to gain valuable insights into weather patterns, temperature variations, and rainfall across the continent. The visualizations presented below cover the years 2007 to 2017 and provide a comprehensive understanding of the climate during this period.
